Using Aikuma is easy!

  1. To start just select new project. Give your project a name and optionally fill in some details such as language and speakers but you can come back and edit these later.
  2. Now you can can record right now, or you can import a recording you made earlier.
  3. On the project screen you can decide what to do from here, e.g. make or import another recording such as a translation, or get to annotating the recording.
