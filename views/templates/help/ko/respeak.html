Recording a respeaking with Aikuma-NG

In this view you can perform a step-by-step respeaking of the recording. This is helpful if the source is not very clear or if you want to record a more fluid version of the source recording. It's particularly helpful when it comes to producing text annotations because you can listen to the source, the respoken audio or both when performing an annotation.

The respeaking process is performed by first listening to a part of the source and then recording you as you slowly and clearly repeat what you heard.

First press and Hold Left Ctrl or click and Hold the button to play the source, then press and Hold Right Ctrl or click and Hold the button to record your respeaking.

Repeat until you have respoken everything you want to. Your work is being saved as you go and you can return later to continue your work.

When playing, you can press and Hold Left Ctrl again to rewind and listen again. Press and Hold ← or → to skip back and fast forward. You can't use this to listen again.*

Made a mistake? Press Escor click to delete the last respeaking so you can listen again and repeat. To delete the entire respeaking click the the button.

To finish, click the button to return to the session view.

* Note: You can listen to the whole respeaking on the session view and you can continue working on the respeaking later.
