Recording a translation with Aikuma-NG

In this view you can perform a step-by-step translation of the recording. This is done by first listening to a part of the source and then recording a translation with a microphone on your computer.

Note: You can also use this view to record something other than a translation such as general commentary.

First press and Hold Left Ctrl or click and Hold the button to play the source, then press and Hold Right Ctrl or click and Hold the button to record your translation.

Repeat until you have translated everything you want to. Your work is being saved as you go and you can return later.

When playing, you can press and Hold Left Ctrl again to rewind and listen again. Press and Hold ← or → to skip back and fast forward. You can't use this to listen again.*

Made a mistake? Press Esc or click to delete the last translation so you can listen again and repeat. To delete the entire translation click the the button.

To finish, enter the translation language in the language box and click the button to return to the session view.

* You can listen to the whole translation on the session view and you can continue working on the translation later.
