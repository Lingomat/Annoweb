Annotating with Aikuma-NG

There are two annotation modes. The first is if you have no regions marked and wish to annotate as-you-go. The second is when you are working inside regions*.

  1. Hold Ctrl to play and mark. Hold Ctrl again to rewind and repeat hear and mark. Press Return after typing an annotation, then Hold Ctrl again to mark the next region.

    Hold ← or →to skip back or fast forward. Press Esc to remove marked region.

  2. Press Ctrl to play region. If you have respeaking/translation, you will hear this too. Press Ctrl again to hear again. Press Return after typing an annotation, then press Ctrl again to play the next region.

    Press ← or →to skip to previous or next region. Press Esc to remove the last region.

Other keys

Press ↑ or ↓ to move between annotations. Press Tab to switch between tracks.

Joining tracks

* If you have recorded respeaking or translated audio, press the track icon on the right of the annotation, such as What to join respeaking/translation. You will then be able to listen to the the source and the respeaking/translation.
